Flappy Bird's gone turbo!

No Java?!

A Flappy Bird spin-off developed by myself and two classmates almost two years ago. If you're having trouble running it, check to make sure your browser has enabled Java (more info on that here ). If that fails, you can enable it through Control Panel as is documented here. Once it loads, begin by hitting either the 'Rules' button. Or, if you'd rather not wade through a seemingly infinite amoung of tedious text, click on 'Play' and get Faby going by pressing the spacebar. Enjoy!
